Nutrition Guidance

Dietary suggestions to support your mental wellness journey

Brain-Boosting Foods

  • Omega-3 rich fish (salmon, mackerel)
  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Berries (blueberries, strawberries)
  • Nuts and seeds (walnuts, chia seeds)
  • Dark chocolate (70%+ cacao)

Energy Sustainers

  • Complex carbs (quinoa, sweet potatoes)
  • Lean proteins (chicken, tofu, legumes)
  • Healthy fats (avocado, olive oil)
  • Greek yogurt with probiotics
  • Green tea for steady energy

Stress-Fighting Foods

  • Magnesium-rich foods (almonds, spinach)
  • Vitamin C sources (citrus, bell peppers)
  • Adaptogenic herbs (ashwagandha, turmeric)
  • Herbal teas (chamomile, passionflower)
  • Fermented foods (kimchi, kefir)

Weekly Nutrition Tips

Hydration for Mental Clarity

Aim for 8-10 glasses of water daily. Even mild dehydration can affect mood and cognitive function.

Meal Timing Matters

Eat balanced meals every 3-4 hours to maintain stable blood sugar and energy levels.

Mindful Eating

Practice eating without distractions to improve digestion and satisfaction with meals.
